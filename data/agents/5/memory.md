## ASSET 2 — COLD CALL SCRIPT
*Best call windows: 10 AM–12 PM or 2–4 PM. Never during bar rush (5–8 PM).*

---

### OPENER

> **"Hey, is [Owner Name] around?"**

**If gatekeeper asks who's calling:**
> **"It's [Your Name] with Nic at Nite Vending — it's about putting some extra revenue in the bar. Quick two-minute call. Is [Owner Name] the right person?"**

If owner's unavailable: *"No problem — what's the best way to reach [him/her] directly?"* Get a cell number, log it, move on.

---

### THE PITCH (45–60 seconds)

> **"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with Nic at Nite Vending — we're a Wyoming-based company and we place vape vending machines in bars and venues at zero cost to the owner. You keep a commission on every sale, we handle all the stocking and maintenance. The reason I'm calling is I'm placing a machine in [their area] and wanted to see if [Venue Name] might be a fit before I go to the place down the street.**
>
> **I'm not trying to sell you anything — just a two-minute conversation to see if the numbers make sense for you. Is now an okay time?"**

*Pause. Let them respond. Do not keep talking.*

---

### IF THEY WANT TO HEAR MORE

> **"Perfect. We put the machine in, we own it, we stock it, we fix it if it breaks. Zero on your end. You earn a commission on every sale — we're talking [$150–$500/month] depending on your traffic. I'd rather show you in person than explain it over the phone — takes about 10 minutes. Are you around [Day 1] or [Day 2] this week?"**

Go for the meeting. Don't try to close over the phone.

---

### OBJECTION HANDLING

| They Say | You Say |
|---|---|
| **"We already sell vapes behind the bar"** | "That's perfect — means your customers are already buying. The machine catches sales after hours and when you're three-deep at the bar. It adds to what you're already selling, doesn't replace it. Worth a 10-minute chat?" |
| **"Not interested"** | "Fair enough. Can I ask — is it the machine itself or just not the right time? Most owners I talk to had the same reaction before they saw the commission check. Mind if I check back in a few months?" |
| **"Send me something"** | "Absolutely — what's the best number to text you? I'll send a one-pager tonight and follow up in a day or two to make sure you got it." |
| **"What about age verification?"** | "The machine is 21+ only with built-in age verification. We only place in venues that are already 21+ — so being a bar already covers you. No new compliance burden on your end." |
| **"What if it breaks?"** | "We fix it. It's our machine — you don't touch it." |
| **"What if nobody buys?"** | "30-day trial — if it doesn't perform, I take it out. You've lost nothing." |

---

> **"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] from Nic at Nite Vending — Wyoming-based company. We place vape machines in venues like [Venue Name] that earn the owner passive income with zero work on their end. I'm placing a machine in [city] soon and wanted to give you first shot. Call me back at [number] — takes two minutes. Talk soon."**

## ASSET 3 — 3-TOUCH FOLLOW-UP TEXT SEQUENCE
*Use after any first contact where you didn't close.*

---

| Touch | Timing | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Touch 1** | 24–48 hours after first contact | Re-open, stay top of mind |
| **Touch 2** | 5–7 days later (no response) | Add urgency + new hook |
| **Touch 3** | 10–14 days later (still no response) | Final nudge, easy yes/no |

---

### TOUCH 1 — 24–48 Hours

**After a walk-in or brief conversation:**
> Hey [Name], it's [Your Name] from Nic at Nite Vending — stopped by [Venue Name] the other day. Wanted to follow up on the vape machine. Zero cost to you, you earn a commission every month. Happy to bring one by and show you how it works. Good day this week? 🤙

**After sending a flyer:**
> Hey [Name], [Your Name] from Nic at Nite — sent over that info about the vape machine. Short version: we put it in, stock it, fix it — you just collect the commission. Worth a quick chat? I can come by [Day] or [Day].

**After a cold call where they said "not right now":**
> Hey [Name], [Your Name] from Nic at Nite. Totally get if timing wasn't right — just wanted to leave the door open. Zero cost to you, monthly commission, Wyoming-local company. If you ever want to explore it, I'm easy to reach. No pressure. 👍

---

### TOUCH 2 — 5–7 Days (No Response)

**Social proof angle:**
> Hey [Name], checking back in on the vape machine — just placed one at a bar in [nearby city] and they're already seeing solid numbers. Wanted to make sure you had first shot in [their city] before I fill the spots. Good time to talk this week?

**Revenue hook:**
> [Name] — [Your Name] again from Nic at Nite. Based on your location and foot traffic, I'd estimate [Venue Name] could earn $[X]–$[X]/month from the machine. No obligation — just want you to have the real number before you decide. Want me to swing by?

**Low-friction ask:**
> Hey [Name] — still thinking about the vape machine or is now not the time? Either answer is totally fine — just want to know if I should keep [Venue Name] on my list. 🙏

---

### TOUCH 3 — 10–14 Days (Still No Response)

**Competitor nudge:**
> Hey [Name], last check-in from [Your Name] at Nic at Nite. Got a machine ready to place in [city] and wanted to give [Venue Name] first shot before I go to someone nearby. The commission is there whether it's you or the next guy — just thought you'd want the option. Worth 10 minutes?

**The "yes or no is fine" close:** *(highest converter)*
> [Name] — [Your Name] from Nic at Nite, last follow-up I promise 😄 Still interested in a vape machine at zero cost to you? Just reply YES and I'll set up a quick visit, or NO and I'll stop bugging you. Either way — Wyoming's a small place, hope we cross paths. 🤝

**Long-game / brand close:**
> Hey [Name] — wrapping up my Nic at Nite follow-ups for [month]. Not going to keep pinging you, but if you ever want a no-cost revenue stream from a Wyoming-local company, you know where to find me. Take care. — [Your Name] 🏔️

---

### TEXTING BEST PRACTICES
- **Always say your name + company** — they haven't saved your number
- **3 sentences max** — if they scroll, it reads like a sales blast
- **One question per text** — multiple questions = zero answers
- **Personalize every message** — name + venue name, not a blast
- **Best windows:** Tue–Thu, 10 AM–12 PM or 2–5 PM
- **Never follow up after an explicit "no"** — that's harassment
